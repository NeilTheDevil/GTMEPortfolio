GTM Tooling (Smartlead, Clay) High-Precision Targeting / Nearshoring Use Case

Hiring Intent &
Signal-Based Outbound

Architecting outbound systems that trigger based on real hiring signals and intent data — ensuring every message lands at exactly the right moment.

3× Reply Rate Lift
~70% Less Manual Research
Clay + Smartlead Core Stack
01 — The Problem

Spray-and-pray outbound was killing conversion

Most outbound teams were sending cold emails to static lists with no real signal of buying intent. The result was low reply rates, irrelevant messaging, and SDR teams burning pipeline on companies that had no immediate need.

For nearshoring businesses in particular, the timing window is extremely narrow — a company actively opening roles they can't fill locally is a high-intent signal. But capturing that signal in real time, at scale, required a different kind of infrastructure.

02 — The System

A real-time trigger engine built on hiring data

01

Signal Detection

Clay tables are configured to pull real-time job postings from LinkedIn, Indeed, and company career pages. Filters are set to surface specific roles that indicate a nearshoring window (e.g., "Sales Development Rep" or "Data Entry Analyst" postings in high-cost markets).

02

Enrichment & Scoring

Each triggering company is enriched via Claygent and third-party data providers. Fields pulled: headcount growth velocity, tech stack, decision-maker contact, and open role count. A compound score determines outreach priority.

03

Personalised Sequencing

High-scoring leads push directly into Smartlead with dynamically generated first lines that reference the specific role they are hiring for and the cost delta of nearshoring that role. No generic copy. Every email is contextually relevant.

04

CRM Sync & Routing

Replies and positive signals are automatically routed to the CRM with enriched data attached — no manual copy-paste. SDRs receive a pre-warmed lead with full context instead of a raw contact.

03 — The Stack

Tools & Technologies

Clay Signal detection, enrichment & orchestration
Smartlead Multi-channel outbound sequencing
Claygent (AI) Autonomous company research & enrichment
LinkedIn / Job APIs Real-time hiring signal source
CRM (HubSpot) Lead routing & pipeline management
n8n / Webhooks Automation glue & trigger orchestration
04 — The Outcome

Measurable results, not vanity metrics

By switching from list-based cold outreach to intent-triggered precision targeting, the client saw a significant improvement in reply rates and qualified meeting bookings. More importantly, the system reduced the time SDRs spent on manual research by roughly 70%, allowing them to focus on conversations rather than data work.

The nearshoring pitch landed harder because it was delivered at exactly the right moment — when the company was already feeling the cost of filling those roles locally. Timing, as it turns out, is the most important form of personalisation.

"The difference between a cold email and the right email at the right time is the entire gap between a 1% and a 12% reply rate."
